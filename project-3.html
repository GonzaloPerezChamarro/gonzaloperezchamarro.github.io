Dead Science

Dead Science is a first person exploration and puzzles game in which the player is a woman who investigates a case of multiple dissapearance.

Project Overview

Dead Science is a first person exploration and puzzles game where the player takes on the role of a woman investigating the mysterious disappearance of the Blair family. The story blends science fiction and mystery, with progressively complex puzzles. The game’s core mechanic is a special visor that allows players to explore alternate dimensions layered over reality.

This project was developed during my final year of university with some classmates. The main idea was to create a very ambitious project. On the one hand, to achieve a hyper-realistic aesthetic with close attention to atmosphere and lighting. On the other hand, to create entertaining gameplay with simple mechanics to achieve an immersive experience.

My role on the project involved programming management, from organizing and distributing tasks within the department to addressing the technical challenges of Unreal Engine.

Development took approximately one year, during which time it was showcased at video game events such as Madrid Games Week 2018, Fun&Serious 2018, ZeroUno, and IndieMad 2018. The project was also presented to publishers and investors.

Partners: Nicolás Tapia, Alberto Guzmán, Javier Tiradas, Alfredo Pérez.
