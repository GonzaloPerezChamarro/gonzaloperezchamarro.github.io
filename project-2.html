Tequila Works Project

This project is an open-world narrative action adventure, developed by Tequila Works, focused on human emotions as the driving force of gameplay.

Project Overview

Project developed by Tequila Works, under NDA.

I worked here for 2+ years as Artificial Intelligence Programmer, developing deep behaviors for several NPCs and creatures of this open world game. In addiction, I contributed to improve an AI framework.

The field of AI on this project ranged behavior trees, perception system, smart objects, utility decision system, etc. Also, Unreal's Gameplay Ability System (GAS) was used in this development.
